Chicken Burrito

Description

This chicken burrito recipe is a quick, satisfying meal made with store-bought flour tortillas, seasoned chicken, rice, beans, and all your favorite fillings. It’s perfect for lunch or dinner and can be customized to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 2 store-bought large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken (grilled or rotisserie)
  • ½ cup cooked rice (preferably Mexican or seasoned)
  • ½ cup canned black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar or Mexican cheese blend
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup salsa or pico de gallo
  • 1 small avocado or guacamole (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • Salt, pepper, and taco seasoning (to taste)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and warm up the shredded chicken. Season it with salt, pepper, and taco seasoning. Set aside.
  2. Warm the tortillas on a dry skillet or microwave them for 15 seconds until soft and pliable.
  3. Lay the tortilla flat and layer the center with rice, black beans, seasoned chicken, cheese, salsa, sour cream, and avocado if using.
  4. Fold the bottom of the tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll tightly into a burrito shape.
  5. Place the burrito seam-side down on a hot skillet and toast for 1–2 minutes per side until golden brown and sealed.
  6. Slice in half and serve warm with extra salsa or sour cream on the side.
