Pizza

Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim – and it puts the commercial pizzas to shame.

Ingredients

  • 1 store-bought pizza crust (pre-baked or ready-to-bake)
  • ½ cup pizza sauce (or use marinara or crushed tomatoes with some seasoning)
  • 150g fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced or torn)
  • 1-2 fresh tomatoes (thinly sliced, optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (about 8-10)
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt (a pinch, optional)
  • Crushed black pepper (optional)
  • Dried oregano or Italian seasoning (optional for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F), or follow the instructions on your pizza crust packaging.
  2. Place the store-bought pizza crust on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  3. Spread ½ cup of pizza sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border.
  4. Layer mozzarella slices evenly on top of the sauce. Optionally, add thin tomato slices.
  5. Season with salt, crushed pepper, and oregano. Drizzle olive oil over the top.
  6. Bake the pizza in the oven for 8-12 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and crust is golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Let the pizza rest for 1-2 minutes, then slice and serve hot!
